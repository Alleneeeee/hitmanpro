About HitmanPro

HitmanPro is designed to be a second-opinion malware scanner, meaning it works alongside your primary antivirus solution. It's lightweight, doesn't require installation, and utilizes cloud technology for malware detection and behavior analysis. The program offers two primary versions: HitmanPro (free scan) and HitmanPro.Alert (real-time protection).

Understanding HitmanPro Licensing

HitmanPro offers single-device and multi-device licenses. Customers can choose from one-year or three-year subscription plans. After purchasing, a license key is emailed to you, which you can activate from within the software. Always store your key in a safe place or create an account on the HitmanPro portal to manage it.

How to Activate HitmanPro

  • Download and install HitmanPro or HitmanPro.Alert
  • Open the program and go to the activation section
  • Enter your purchased license key
  • Click Activate and wait for confirmation
  • Your software is now licensed for full use
